Career Objective

Overall I am huge advocate of globalizing access to education and creating the resources need for affordable interdisciplinary learning paths. My mission has been to create communities that share and build locally, and globally. The organizations encompassing these ideals the most have manifested as makerspaces. In 2012 I came across the MIT professors Neil Gershenfeld’s “The beckoning promise of personal fabrication” video, and ever since I have been on mission to create a global revolution through technology access and education.
